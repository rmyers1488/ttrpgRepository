Jamison, Special Affect Magi

Age 24, Height: 5’4”, weight 117 lbs, hair red, eyes blue, sex male, ethnicity european/asian,.

Jamison dropped out of high school, got a job working as a propman for the local theater. He progressed into the effects for the productions. From there he wound up working for low budget films as a special effects man. He has gone from apprentice to journeyman.

What's more is that Jamison stumbled upon a well kept secret. An older woman named Nancy, who acts in the local theater scene, is a witch! When he discovered her secret he freaked out. Not certain with how to act, whether he should freeze from shock or elation, he quickly descended into a blabbering mess. After he recovered from his embarrassing state he tried to convince Nancy to teach him her arcane secrets. Needless to say he eventually succeeded.

Three years have passed and Jamison has become a more than competent mage. He now works as a part time mercenary for the local powers. Handling work that ranges from dealing with the undead or transporting valuable cargo he handles it all.
