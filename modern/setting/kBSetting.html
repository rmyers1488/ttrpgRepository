Based on the webcomic Kiwi Blitz

After what was known as the third world war it took the world years to recover. The final act of the madness was the detonation of nuclear weapons in several large cities through the world. In several countries rebellions, violent protests, and natural disasters caused great damage.

Now, 27 years after the end of the 3rd World War, the world has stabilized and some areas are stronger then before.

Basic international law includes advanced AI being illegal. In most countries genetic modification, beyond what would be medically necessary, ranges from frowned upon to outright illegal.

Here in the USA cop shootings of unarmed civilians gave rise the adaption of non-lethal weapons. Firearms that only fire stun rounds. These rounds transfers the energy to the target by deforming upon impact instead of penetrating.

At a certain level genetic engineering is legal. However there is a geneticist who went rouge and actually started a criminal organization to fund his research. The members of his group are typically altered, often with anamalistic traits that range from dog ears or short coat of fur to the more extensive with muzzles or tails or some times with more full body alterations.

The third World War has rendered most of Californa, Nevada & Utah uninhabited with the western half of Utah and third of Ariozna are likewise damaged. Florida, Georgia, and South Carolina were hit hard in WW III.
