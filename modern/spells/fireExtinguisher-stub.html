Fire Extinguisher

School Evocation; Level Arcane 2. Divine 2
casting
Casting Time Attack Action
Components V, S
effect
Range close (25 ft. + 5 ft/2 levels)
Effect cloud of CO2, spreads 30 ft and is 5 ft high Duration1 minute/level
Saving Throw none; Spell Resistance none
description

The spell Fire Extinguisher works by creating a low hanging cloud of CO2. This will snuff out any open flame and hinder new flames. Any open flame in the area of the spell is extinguised. Any fire based attacks half damage.
