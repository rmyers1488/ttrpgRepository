Campsite

School Conjuration; Level Mage 3
casting
Casting Time 10 minutes
Components V, S, M (a scrap of a tent, a piece of bark, and ash from a firepit)
effect
Range close (25 ft. + 5 ft/2 levels)
Effect 10-ft square campsight Duration 12 hours
Saving Throw none; Spell Resistance none
description

This spell conjurs forth a simple campground composing of a tent sized for one or two people, a small, short, table or stump with a propain stove and a set of camp cooking supplies. The campground lacks groundcover and also has a small fire pit.
