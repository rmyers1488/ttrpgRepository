Homeless Encampment

School Conjuration; Level Mage 3
casting
Casting Time 10 minutes
Components V, S, M (a scrap of a tent, a chip of a sidewalk, and a scrap of old clothing)
effect
Range close (25 ft. + 5 ft/2 levels)
Effect 10-ft square encampment Duration12 hours
Saving Throw none; Spell Resistance none
description

This spells creates, for all intents and purposes, a homeless encampment in a ten foot square area. The encampment created is appropriate for the environments and includes, at a minimum, a small shelter suitable for one or two people, rudimentary cooking supplies (enough to make a simple meal), a stool, and some waste material such as newspapers and plastic bags.
