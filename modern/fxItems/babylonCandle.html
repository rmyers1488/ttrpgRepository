Babylon Candle

A Babylon Candle allows for the holder to make three strides once lit. Each stride allows you to cover up to 100 miles. Once lit a Babylon Candle cannot be put out and will burn for ten minutes. All three strides must be made while the candle is lit.
Type: Wondrous Item (magic); Caster Level: 10th; Purchase DC: 31; Weight: -.
