Scorpian's Glove

Price 20,000g; Slot Hands; CL 12th Weight -; Aura moderate necromancy

description

This finely made silk glove functions as a +1 Spellstoring weapon for touch attacks. The glove can be poisoned as a swift action that requires a free hand. It also appears to be a normal glove. Only true seeing or similar magic to reveil its true natures.
Any creature struck by a scorpian's glove suffers a -4 penalty on saving throws against the poison applied to the glove. The frequency of any poison applied to the weapon increases by 2. Attempts to cure the poisone target with a skill or magic takes a -4 penalty.

construction requirments

Cost 10,000g; Feats Craft Magic Arms and Armor; death knell, pernicious poison
