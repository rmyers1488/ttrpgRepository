Pepperbox Pistol, Coat

This small pepperbox pistol follows the same principle as the coat pistol. It is both smaller and less powerful than a standard size pepperbox pistol. It's compact size makes it easier to conceal on your body, a +2 bonus to Sleight of Hand.
A coat pepperbox has only four barrels instead of the six of a regular sized pepperbox. A coat pepper box's barrels are small enough and back far enough to be rotated using your thumb allowing for a second weapon to be used.

Pepperbox Pistol, Light

This pepperbox pistol is lighter than a regular pepperbox pistol.

Pistol, Giant

This flintlock pistol has a single, massive barrel. The massive bullet that it fires deals massive damage, and produces a massive kick. A giant pistol suffers a -6 equipment penalty to ATK rolls, a -2 penalty to ATK rolls if you have a STR score of eighteen or above.

Double Hackbut, Rifled

A rifled Double Hackbut follows all the rules for the normal Double Hackbut. The carrage must be set up or you suffer a -4 attack penalty and are nocked prone. But it also has 10 range increments as all rifled flintlocks, but it also takes 2 full-round actions to reload it.

Calvary Musket

Also known as a carbine it is just as dangerous as a normal musket but as its barrel is shorter it has less range. It is the perfected weapon of mounted Calvary, dragoons, and thrifty gunslingers alike. The barrel of a carbine isn't much more then two feet in length the musket can be hidden in long coats and thick skirts. Unlike regular muskets that take a -12 penalty to conceal on your person a carbine has a -8 penalty.

Heavy Musket

A simple change to the basic musket; and although most soldiers dislike it for its unfavorable weight but love the increase lethallity. Dwarven infantry will rarely use a lighter one.

Shield Dragon

This Light Steel Shield is outfitted with a Dragon Pistol. It is considered to be both a shield and a weapon for the purposes of creating masterwork or magical versions of this weapon. You must enchant them sepperatly. If this firearm gains the broken condition, both the firearm component and the sword are considered broken. You must remove the Shield to reload the pistol. Because of its awkward construction, a shield dragon is always suffers a -1 penalty to attack rolls.

Hand Mortar

This is quite possibly the most insane weapon every developed. While brilliant in concept, it is sometimes seen as suicidal. A hand mortar is loaded by shoving a lit grenade down the barrel, hence the suicidal part. Loading and firing a hand mortar is a full-round action. If a hand mortar misfires there is a 20% chance that it explods. If you have the feat Exotic Weapon Seige Firearms you may make indirect attacks with a hand mortar like with a catapult. If you do not have the feat then you suffer a -5 penalty to the Intelligence check.

Long Mortar

A long morter functions as a hand mortar though with greater range.

When unfolded the pepperbox points backward when used as brass knuckles. It takes an action to remove the knuckles and turn it around to use either the bayonet or the revolver. when the revolver, bayonet and brass knuckels are folded in it has a +4 bonus to conealing it on your person. As a Pugno lacks any form of a barrel it only has 5 range increments and a -2 penalty on ranged attack rolls. The bayonet deals 1d3 piercing damage with a 20/x2 Critical threat.

Glove Pistol

This weapon allows you to shoot your opponent by pressing the plunger into your opponent. This weapon is fired as part of a melee unarmed attack or as part of a melee touch attack.
a pistol glove can be made as part of a cestus or gauntlet for an extra 3 gp.

Ring Revolver

This is a heavy ring with a rathe rlarge head. In reality it is an incredibly tiny, and weak, firearm. A ring revolver only has 3 range increments and a -2 penalty on attack rolls. It grants a +2 bonus to slight of hand checks to hide it on your person by turning it around.
The only ones that normally favor this unpowered weapon are assassins who use poisons.

Halverd Rifle

This weapon combines a rifle with a halberd.

Boar Spear Shotgun

The length of the boar spear makes aiming the shotgun half of this weapon more difficult. It suffers a -2 penalty to attack rolls. If you ready the boar spear portion against a charge and your attack hits, you not only gain a +2 shield bonus to your against that creature until your next turn, but you may also make an attack with shotgun against that creature as a free action. This attack automatically hits and is a critical threat.

Any revolver can be made to use percussion caps. Cost, range, weight are the same, the only difference is that it uses lose powder, a bullet & a percussion cap. While it takes a standard action to load each cylinder the fact that it does not use metal cartridges means that ammunition can be made in the field with only a few basic supplies.

Clutch Gun

A small purse similar to ones used by ladies on a night out with friends that is actually a modified pistol. The trigger is connected to a clasp or something similar while the barrel is hidden behind an easily replaceable panel. As it cannot be properly aimed a Clutch Gun has a -2 penalty to Attack rolls. The clutch itself can be made to serve as a clutch but has severely limited space.

"Sword" Rifle

This weapon appears to be a longsword with a slightly curved hilt. In truth is a light rifle hidden in its sheath, not a sword. It takes a Perception DC 20 check to notice that it is a rifle while it is in its sheath, DC 5 if handled.

"Dagger" Pistol

While a Rifle “Sword” is a sheathed rifle a Pistol “Dagger” is a sheathed dagger. As with a rifle “sword” it takes a Perception DC 20 check to notice that it is a pistol when sheathed.

"Sword" Pistol

Instead of a sheathed dagger this pistol appears to be a sheathed short sword and takes a Perception DC 20 check to notice that it is pistol when sheathed.

Salt Shot Alchemical Cartridge

This mix of black powder and rock salt can only be loaded into a blunderbuss, a dragon pistol, or other scatter weapon. It deals nonlethal instead of lethal damage, and increases the misfire value by 1. You can only use it with a scatter weapon’s cone attack.

Burning Salt Alchemical Cartridge

This salt alcemical cartridge both deals nonleathal but also deals 1d6 points of fire damage. And any creature struck must make a DC 20 REF SV or catch on fire.

Entangling Burning Salt Alchemical Cartridge

This alchemical cartridge contains the materials for and entangling shot and alchemical powders that burn at a high temperature. It not only functions as an entangling shot alchemical cartridge, but it deals 1d6 points of fire damage as well. And any creature struck must make a DC 20 REF SV or catch on fire. If it catches on fire, for the next 2 rounds extinguishing the flames is a DC 25 Reflex save instead of a DC 15 save, and using water to extinguish the flames creates a burst of burning material equivalent to alchemist's fire making a direct hit on the target (including splash damage). After the initial 2 rounds, the flames may be extinguished as normal.

Holy Salt Alchemical Cartridge

A special version of the Salt Shot Cartridge that is created using blessed salt. Against undead and evil outsiders it deals lethal damage while against other creature types it deals nonlethal damage, it will also overcome DR as a good aligned weapon. This Cartridge increases the misfire value by 1 and can only be used with a scatter weapon’s cone attack.

Smoke Shot Alchemical Cartridge

This alchemical cartridge creates a cloud of foul but harmless yellow smoke. The smoke shot works as a smokestick except that the smoke only fills a 5’ square and lasts for 1 round before dispersing.

Thunderstone Alchemical Cartridge

Each creature within a 10’ radius spread must make a DC 12 Fortitude save or be deafened for 1 hour. This Cartridge can only be made as shotgun slugs. Increase the Misfire value by 1.
