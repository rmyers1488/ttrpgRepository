The Governess

A Governess is a teacher of young children and a chaperone of older girls and young women.
A Governess is also a guardian of her charges. Highly skilled with the pistol and knife they are fiercly protecteve of their charges. This combination of skills ensures that they are sought out by the upperclass.

Requirments: female only

Skills

All knowledge skills, remove climb and survival.
6 + Int skill points per level

A Ladies Armaments

A Governess is proficient with light armor, light firearms, light simple weapons, Butterfly Sword, Cane Sword, Dueling Dagger, Fighting Fan, Kerambit, Short Sword, Switchblade Knife, Wakizashi and Wushu Dart. She is only proficient in handwrapps made as lady's gloves.

Deeds

The governess gains the following deeds.

Interpose (Ex): at 1st level the gunsling capable of protecting her charges. When an attack is made against an adjacent ally she may spend 1 grit to grant a +1 bonus to armor class and reflex saves. Increase the bonus by +1 every 7th level and every 6 levels to a maximum of +4 at 19th level. She may spend 2 grit points instead to grant twice the bonus to armor class and reflex saves.

This deed replaces Gunslinger's Dodge.

Well Educated (Ex): At third level a governess may take a move action to spend 1 grit point to make a DC 15 knowledge check. On a successful knowledge check she deals +1 damage against that opponent and increases her critical range by 1. She deals an additional +1 damage at 7th level and an additional +1 damage ever four levels thereafter to a maximum of +5 at 19th level. She may only use this deed against one oppenent at a time.

This deed replaces Pistol Whip

Skilled Governess

A governess with your skills are in great demand. At 5th level she gains the Signature Skill: Profession(Governess) as a bonus feat.

Pocket Gun Training(Ex)

Starting at 5th level, a governess gains a bonus to damage with light firearms equal to her dexterity bonus. She also gains a +2 bonus to conceal light firearms on her person with a further +2 bonus every 4 leves thereafter (9th, 13th, and 17th) to a maximum of +8. Furthermore when she misfires with a light firearm the misfire value of the firearm increases by 2 instead of 4.

This modifies Gun Training
