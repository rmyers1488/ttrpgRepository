Elenore, the Enchanting Courtesian

Most pretty young girls grow up in loving households and merry decent young men.

Elenore is not most pretty young girls and certainly did not grow up in a loving household. At a young age she was taken from her home and forced into slavery. Now being a half-elf in a culture that feared elves meant that she was subjected to more cruelty then her peers. Once she entered puberty it was almost certain that she would be raped.

Thankfully for her she was purchased from her previous owner by the owner of a certain brothel. Elenore believed that nothing would change but, everything did. Her new master had different plans in mind for her.

Her new master, one Tilvia Ashertil, was no ordinary woman. As a multi-century old vampiress, possibly millenia old vampiress, she rules a vast business empire and steers politics as a hobby. And she has a son. A young swordsman who was named Ahmeen but calls himself Joseph.

Tilvia’s greatest desire is grandchildren but Joseph is determined to not give her any. That is where Elenore comes in. Tilvia has raised that girl into a woman with charm, grace, and strength. She has raised her with the sole purpose of Elenore becoming the mother of her grandchildren.

With an education consisting of a noblewoman’s skills, the cortesian arts, magic, and the more subtle arts of assassination. This training has turned a meek slave fearful of the quip into a confident young woman capable of holding her own in any setting.

Joining Joseph as an adventurer she has found that she rather enjoys trasping about and dealing with bandits and collecting rewards for dealing with monsters. Now if she could only convince him about that baby.
