Species Traits

Size: Medium fey
Ability Modifiers: +2 CHA, +2 DEX, +2 WIS
Extra Starting Hit Dice: A Yuki-Onna gains 2 Hit Dice (2d6 hit points). The Yuki-Onna's Constitution modifiers applies to each Hit Dice when determining hit points.
Base Speed: 30 feet
Attack Bonus: Yuki-Onna gain a +1 species bonus on attack rolls.
Low-Light Vision: Yuki-Onna can see twice as far as a human in starlight, moonlight, torchlight, and similar conditions of poor illumination. They retain the ability to distinguish color and detail under these conditions.
Free Language Skills: Yuki-Onnas speek Japanese
Bonus Feat: Archaic Weapon Proficiency or Simple Weapon Proficiency
Immunities: Yuki-Onnas are immune to cold energy
Weakness: Yuki-Onnas are weak to fire energy
Aura of Cold (Su): A Yuki-Onna creates an aura of intense cold. Living Creatures within 20 feet must make a Fort SV DC 10 + Consititution modifer, or take 1d4 points of nonlethal damage.
Frost Touch (Su): A Yuki-Onna deals one additional point of cold damage on melee attacks.
Level Adjustment: +2.
